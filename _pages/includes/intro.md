# 😸 About Me
He is currently a Ph.D. student in the [School of Computer Science and Engineering](https://cse.sysu.edu.cn) at [Sun Yat-sen University](https://www.sysu.edu.cn/sysuen) <img src='./images/sysu_logos_nobg.png' style='width: 6em;'> (中山大学计算机学院), supervised by [Prof. Xiaohua Xie (谢晓华教授)](https://cse.sysu.edu.cn/content/2478), and also co-supervised by [Prof. Jianhuang Lai (赖剑煌教授)](https://cse.sysu.edu.cn/content/2498).

His research interests include *3D reconstruction*, *medical image processing*, *video/salient object segmentation*, and *anomaly detection*. He has published some papers at the top international CV conferences and journals, such as **ICCV**, **CVPR**, **IEEE TIP**, **IEEE TCSVT**. He has served as a reviewer of **CVPR 2023**, **ECCV 2022** and **IEEE TCSVT**.
