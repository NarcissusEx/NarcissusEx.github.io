He is currently a PHD student in the [School of Computer Science and Engineering](https://cse.sysu.edu.cn) at [Sun Yat-sen University](https://www.sysu.edu.cn/sysuen) <img src='./images/sysu_logos_nobg.png' style='width: 6em;'> (中山大学计算机学院), supervised by [Prof. Xiaohua Xie (谢晓华教授)](https://cse.sysu.edu.cn/content/2478), and also co-supervised by [Prof. Jianhuang Lai (赖剑煌教授)](https://cse.sysu.edu.cn/content/2498).
